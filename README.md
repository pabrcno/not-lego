## âœ¨ What is this?

**Not LEGO** is an interactive 3D building experience that brings the joy of brick-by-brick construction to your browser with **realistic physics simulation**. Watch your bricks fall, stack, and settle naturally as gravity and collision detection bring your creations to life. Build structures that obey the laws of physics - balance matters, stability is key, and every brick placement feels satisfyingly real. No instruction manual required!
